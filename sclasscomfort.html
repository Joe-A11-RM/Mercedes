Ride comfort highlights

Outstanding suspension comfort.

A large and luxurious limousine like the new Mercedes-Benz S-Class not only offers the maximum ride comfort you would traditionally expect in terms of the suspension and rolling behaviour, its driving characteristics and dynamics have also long been at the top end. This is once again the case with the new S-Class.

×

AIRMATIC

AIRMATIC combines the advantages of several suspensions in one – choose between comfort and dynamism. The set-up range is particularly impressive. The air suspension provides outstanding ride comfort even on difficult roads or a powerfully sporty performance. The adaptive damping system is adjusted separately for each wheel and forms the basis for comfortable, relaxed travel. Automatic level control according to load is a particularly practical feature.

×

Rear axle steering

The rear axle steering with 4.5° steering angle adjustment offers three advantages: even more superior roadholding, significantly enhanced agility and, last but not least, better manoeuvrability – such as when parking.

The rear axle steering with 10° steering angle adjustment reduces the turning circle by almost two metres. As such the new S-Class almost handles like a compact car when manoeuvring. At higher speeds, on the other hand, you drive with the effortless superiority afforded by the long wheelbase.

Active Multicontour Seat Package

The ultimate in seating comfort, even on longer journeys: integrated air cushions provide broad scope for optimising your seating position. AIR-BALANCE Package

The AIR-BALANCE Package provides an individual fragrance in the vehicle interior – in line with your personal preference and mood. Thanks to refreshing ionisation and filtering of the outside and interior air, it also helps to improve air quality and give a greater sense of well-being.

Chauffeur Package

With the Chauffeur Package the front passenger seat can be moved significantly further forward, while its EASY ADJUST luxury head restraint can also be folded down. This allows the rear-seat passenger on this side to benefit from a better view of the road, while they can also rest their weary legs on the fold-out footrest behind the front passenger seat.

Rear ENERGIZING Package

With the Rear ENERGIZING Package, the passengers in the outer seats in the rear also enjoy unique ENERGIZING comfort for all the senses. It combines premium features and pampers you with varied wellness programmes and invigorating fitness functions – for the ultimate in pleasant travel.

Front ENERGIZING Package

Comfort for all the senses: this package bundles together a variety of premium equipment with innovative intelligence. Load Compartment Package

The Load Compartment Package makes it easier for you to load and unload the boot. With HANDS-FREE ACCESS all it takes is a kicking movement underneath the rear apron to open the boot lid. Thanks to the through-loading feature, even bulky items such as skis can be stowed. Remote boot closing at the touch of a button also brings added operating convenience.

×

Memory Package

The Memory Package provides practical ease of operation – especially when your vehicle is used by different people. Besides seat adjustment, the individual position profiles also store the alignment of the steering column and the outside mirrors. Thanks to the fingerprint scanner it is quick and easy to identify yourself.

×

Remote Services Premium *

The Remote Services Premium Package connects you to your vehicle even more closely. If required, you can locate it and control certain functions remotely. Services such as Maintenance Management help with coordinating service appointments and save you valuable time. The tracking of stolen vehicles increases the chances of recovering your property.

* To use the services, you must register on the Mercedes me Portal and also accept the Terms of Use for the Mercedes me connect services. In addition, the respective vehicle must be paired with the user account. Upon expiry of the initial term, the services can be renewed for a fee. First-time activation of the services is possible within 1 year of initial registration or initial operation by the customer, whichever occurs first. Available in U.A.E markets only.
