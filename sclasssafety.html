Highlights

Always here for you.

Thanks to being comprehensively equipped with a range of innovative safety and assistance systems, the new Mercedes-Benz S-Class can provide palpable stress relief and support, while helping to avoid hazardous situations. For example through all-round monitoring of the vehicle's surroundings by the new PRE-SAFE® Plus system.

Frontal airbags for the rear-seat passengers.

Innovative air cushions make travelling in the rear an even safer experience. The rear airbags are integrated into the backrests of the front seats and deploy in the event of a severe front impact. They unfold gently to protect passengers in the outer seats wearing seatbelts against contact with the front seats. In this way the head and neck can be subjected to significantly lower loads.

Only available for the long-wheelbase version of the S-Class.

ATTENTION ASSIST 3.0

The ATTENTION ASSIST drowsiness detection system can detect typical signs of fatigue or growing inattentiveness on long, monotonous journeys, or recognise an impending episode of microsleep on the part of the driver and take action of its own accord to warn them. For this function the S-Class must be configured with the new 3D driver display.

Digital Light

Trailblazing headlamp technology.

DIGITAL LIGHT is optimally adjusted to other road users and to the surrounding conditions at all times. What is more, the HD system harnesses the combined power of more than one million pixels per headlamp to support you in facing any challenge. The high-resolution light can specifically highlight individual areas, project information* onto the road and optimise orientation. All with an unprecedented level of precision at all times.

*Available in certain markets only. Please check availability with your local dealer.
